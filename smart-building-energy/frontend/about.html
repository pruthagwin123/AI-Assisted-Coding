About This Project

This Smart Building Energy Management System is inspired by the IEEE paper "Deep Learning in Energy Modeling: Application in Smart Buildings With Distributed Energy Generation".

Ghadi, Y., et al. "Deep Learning in Energy Modeling: Application in Smart Buildings With Distributed Energy Generation." IEEE Access.

Key Findings Simulated

  • Up to 84% grid import reduction
  • Up to 87% electricity cost savings with coordinated scheduling
  • DWT-LSTM achieved 3.63% MAPE and R2 of 0.99

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, Vanilla JavaScript, Chart.js
  • Backend: Node.js, Express.js
  • Database: MongoDB, Mongoose
  • Security: JWT authentication, bcrypt hashing
  • Data ingestion: Multer CSV upload pipeline
